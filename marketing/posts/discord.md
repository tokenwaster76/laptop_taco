🌮 **Laptop Taco** is live.

It is 2026. Developers are walking around clutching half-open MacBooks because their AI agents will not shut up. I refused.

Tiny macOS CLI that wraps your long-running agent command with `caffeinate`, pings you when it's done.

```
./taco claude
./taco "npm test"
```

No app. No account. No cloud. No subscription. One Bash file. MIT.
