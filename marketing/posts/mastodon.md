It's 2026 and developers are wandering around clutching half-open MacBooks because their AI coding agents refuse to die quietly.

I refused to participate in this lid-ajar nightmare.

So: Laptop Taco 🌮

A single Bash script that wraps your long-running command with macOS `caffeinate`, prints PID + battery, and pings you when the agent is done cooking.

./taco claude
./taco "npm test"

No app. No account. No cloud. No subscription. MIT, ~150 lines, macOS-only.
